About

Built by the 2DPHYS group at DTU

DEX collects the digital experiments and interactive tools developed by the 2DPHYS group at the Technical University of Denmark. Everything runs entirely in the browser — free to use, easy to share, and designed for both research intuition and the classroom. The suite is open and evolving; check back as new simulators come online.
